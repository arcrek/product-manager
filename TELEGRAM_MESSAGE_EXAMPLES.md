## ğŸ’¡ Tips

### 1. Keep It Short
- Headers: 1-2 lines
- Footers: 2-4 lines
- Too long = less readable

### 2. Use Emojis Wisely
- 1-3 emojis per line
- Don't overdo it
- Make it relevant

### 3. Test Before Saving
- Always click "ğŸ“¤ Send Test"
- Check how it looks on mobile
- Verify links work

### 4. Branding Consistency
- Use same header/footer for all messages
- Match your brand colors (via emojis)
- Include key contact info

### 5. Mobile-First
- Most users will see on phone
- Keep lines short
- Use line breaks for readability
